# AVATAR-STYLE-01 R5 Prompt Packet

Status: generation brief
Date: 2026-07-22

## Shared Lock

Create one all-punk lineup in each of the two R4 illustration/sticker styles.
Each sheet contains the same twelve heavily stylized, facially ambiguous,
belly-up characters in the same positions, with substantially different heads,
bodies, hair, and clothing. Generate one shared roster master first and use it
as the identity and layout source for both style renders.

Include a range of punk-adjacent styling such as classic punk, hardcore, crust,
post-punk, goth-punk, ska/Oi, and DIY street-punk without turning the figures
into stereotypes. Include at least three traditional non-racist skinhead/ska/Oi
looks, including:

- one bald or closely shaved character wearing a blank polo shirt with
  suspenders;
- one additional shaved-head character using a different body, skin tone, and
  open outerwear treatment.

Safety and identity constraints:

- no extremist, hate, political, gang, or coded numerical imagery;
- no swastikas, Confederate imagery, runes used as political symbols, or real
  organization marks;
- no real band logos or readable band names;
- preserve six skin tonal values and avoid racialized subculture stereotypes.

## Wearable-Merch Proof

- Keep every jacket, vest, cardigan, and overshirt open.
- Keep the center of every undershirt or polo readable and unobstructed.
- Show original fictional band-like patches using abstract symbols only.
- Show small buttons/pins on at least three different approved host types:
  suspenders, jacket or vest lapels, and hat/beanie surfaces.
- Keep patches and buttons visually separate from the garment so they read as
  attachable digital-merch objects.
- Use no generated words or pseudo-text.

## Palette And Finish

Use black, off-white, and grayscale with one restrained fluorescent
acid-chartreuse accent. Preserve the white die-cut sticker edge around each
assembled figure.

## Review Rule

R5 compares the same focused content in both R4 styles. Neither becomes the
approved base until the founder explicitly selects it.
