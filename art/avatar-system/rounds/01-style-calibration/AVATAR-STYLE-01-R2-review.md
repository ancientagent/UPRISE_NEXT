# AVATAR-STYLE-01 R2 Review

Status: rejected
Date: 2026-07-22

## Founder Decision

The characters are too detailed and particular. As avatars, they must be
heavily stylized and vague or ambiguous in facial features so more users can
recognize themselves in the construction.

## Effect

- No R2 direction is approved or promoted.
- R2 must not be used as the production face-detail level.
- Community difference should be carried primarily by hair, clothing,
  headwear, and optional accessories rather than fixed facial identity.
- The modular system contract remains unchanged.

## R3 Correction

- Use abstract head and jaw silhouettes.
- Reduce eyes, nose, mouth, ears, brows, and facial hair to a small reusable mark
  vocabulary.
- Remove realistic anatomy shading, detailed lips, nostrils, cheekbones,
  eyelashes, wrinkles, and portrait-specific likeness cues.
- Keep skin tones selectable without making them demographic labels.
- Present the figures as expressive paper-doll fit models, not portraits of
  specific people.
