# AVATAR-STYLE-01 R3 Review

Status: direction 3 preferred, not approved
Date: 2026-07-22

## Founder Decision

Direction 3 is the strongest R3 direction, but it does not yet have enough
style. The avatar should feel more like a cohesive illustration and may use a
die-cut sticker language rather than reading as an assembled paper doll.

## R4 Correction

- Preserve heavily stylized, facially ambiguous construction.
- Add stronger authored illustration character and sticker presence.
- Increase variation in head silhouettes, facial-mark combinations, shoulder
  builds, hair, and community styling.
- Use the supplied sticker sheet as inspiration only; do not copy its exact
  characters, clothing, or repeated face structure.
- All jackets, vests, and outerwear must remain open so the undershirt and its
  future printable/digital-merch area remain visible and readable.

Direction 3 remains the parent reference for R4 but is not approved for
production.
