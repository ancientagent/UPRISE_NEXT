# Surface Contract — Community R1

**Status:** `active`
**Owner:** `founder + codex`
**Last Updated:** `2026-04-05`

## Purpose
Define the stable minimum contract for `Community` as the explicit destination for a real scene/community page.

## Core Rule
- `Community` is not a bottom-nav peer to `Home` and `Discover`.
- Community is entered explicitly through community links and `Visit [Community Name]`-style handoff behavior.
- Community identity remains `city + state + music community`.

## Primary Job
- Represent a real place + real music community.
- Surface identity, activity, and participation context for that community.

## Governing Context
- Community identity must never collapse below `city + state + music community`.
- Visitor vs local/Home Scene status remains relevant.
- Visiting a community must not silently mutate Home Scene or civic authority.

## What Must Remain True
- Community pages represent real scene identity, not generic interest groups.
- Visitor-mode boundaries remain respected for non-Home communities.
- Community entry from Discover remains explicit after retune/travel.

## What Community Must Not Become
- not a generic Discord/forum replacement
- not an interest-only group surface
- not a city-only shell with missing music-community identity
- not a place where descriptive metrics become authority

## Known Drift Patterns
- generic community groups
- `Join Community` semantics during Discover travel
- city-only labels
- governance/power inferred from activity metrics

## Open Areas (Not Locked Here)
This contract does not finalize:
- exact layout/card composition for the community page
- deeper governance tooling beyond current visitor/local constraints
- future V2 community-social expansions
