## Clarifications

- Local business is a beta layer in the Home Scene / local music
  infrastructure model.
- Message boards are also a beta layer in the Home Scene / Plot social model.
- These beta layers should be considered when designing the larger thematic
  model for regenerating local music infrastructure.
- This clarification does not activate either layer in the current launch/MVP
  Plot shell.

## Feature Sets

- Local business layer
- Raw basis: "there is a local business layer that will be introduced during beta"
- Included behavior:
  - Treat local businesses as part of the later Home Scene support
    infrastructure.
  - Use the layer to connect local economy, sponsorship, offers/promotions, and
    artist/community support once business-account contracts are active.
  - Keep business behavior scene/locality-bound and non-governing.
- Excluded / not activated:
  - No current MVP Business tab.
  - No current MVP Promotions tab.
  - No billing, paid boost, coupon/offer, sponsor, business dashboard, ranking,
    or Fair Play influence from this note alone.
- Status: beta expansion direction; owner specs and UI/API contracts still
  required.

- Message board layer
- Raw basis: "and a message board as well"
- Included behavior:
  - Treat message boards as a beta social layer for scene-bound discussion.
  - Keep message boards tied to Home Scene/community context rather than a
    generic social feed.
  - Keep message boards distinct from Feed card grammar and BLAST signal
    propagation.
- Excluded / not activated:
  - No current MVP Social tab.
  - No generic DM or artist inbox.
  - No algorithmic discussion ranking.
  - No message-board models/endpoints from this note alone.
- Status: beta expansion direction; social owner spec and moderation contracts
  still required.

## Working Interpretation

The Home Scene visual thesis can show that the local music infrastructure later
has more than music and events: it also grows business support and scene-bound
discussion. For current design and implementation, those layers should be shown
as future/beta architecture only, not as active buttons, tabs, dashboards,
transport controls, or runtime claims inside the current MVP Home/Plot shell.
