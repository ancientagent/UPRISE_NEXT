## Purpose
Record the founder correction for the expanded user profile / collection workspace layout.

## Locked Layout Detail
When the player is pulled down and the user profile / collection workspace opens:
- the profile summary area includes the calendar card
- the collection tab / section selector sits directly below the calendar area
- the active collection section body follows the collection tabs
- the player relocates to the bottom while expanded
- the bottom player may keep normal controls/status if space allows
- the bottom player may shrink to a compact scrolling band / song title marquee if space is tight
- mode, tier, and rotation-pool controls/status remain player-owned and should not be duplicated as a separate profile metadata strip
