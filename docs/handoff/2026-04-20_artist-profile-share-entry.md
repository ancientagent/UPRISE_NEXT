# 2026-04-20 — Artist profile share entry

## What changed
- Added a `Share Artist Page` action to the artist-page header.
- The page now uses the native share sheet when available and falls back to clipboard copy when it is not.

## Why
- Founder direction clarified that the artist page should help users share artist information, not only listen and collect.
- This keeps sharing on the source page itself instead of inventing a signal-style share action on feed cards or song tiles.

## Guardrails preserved
- No wheel on the artist page.
- No `Blast` on the artist page.
- No feed-card inline share action.
- Sharing remains a source-page utility, not a signal/action-row control.
