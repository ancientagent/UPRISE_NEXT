# 2026-03-21 — founder instruction override for philosophy interpretation

## Scope
Locked a session/process rule to prevent future agents from making product decisions based on canon philosophy, narrative framing, or anti-trope interpretation when founder behavior has not been directly confirmed.

## Changes
- Updated `docs/solutions/SESSION_STANDING_DIRECTIVES.md`

## Rule Added
- Do not derive new product behavior from canon philosophy, narrative tone, or abstract product framing alone.
- If the behavior is not explicitly locked in concrete spec/decision language or directly confirmed by founder instruction, escalate instead of interpreting.
- Founder-confirmed behavior overrides philosophical interpretation.

## Reason
Recent UX review surfaced a concrete over-interpretation failure: anti-platform / non-generic-search guidance was interpreted as removing artist search entirely, which exceeded founder-confirmed intent.
